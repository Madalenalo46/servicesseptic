Frequently Asked Questions

Do your portable toilets have lighting for evening or nighttime events?

While our standard units rely on natural light, we do offer optional, battery-operated LED light packs that can be placed inside any unit to provide ample lighting for nighttime use. Our luxury trailers come with full interior and exterior lighting.

Can you place a unit in a fenced-in backyard?

Yes, provided the gate and pathway are wide enough for our equipment. Our units are moved using a heavy-duty dolly, which typically requires a gate opening of at least 48 inches. We can discuss the specifics of your location when you book.

Do we need to provide water or power for the luxury restroom trailers?

For full functionality, our luxury trailers require access to one or two standard electrical outlets and a standard garden hose connection. However, if utilities are unavailable, they can run on their own onboard freshwater tanks and a generator, which we can also provide.

How far in advance do I need to book my rental?

For standard units, we can often accommodate requests with a couple of days' notice. However, for large events, specialty units, or our luxury restroom trailers, we strongly recommend booking several weeks or even months in advance to ensure availability.

Is there a delivery fee for the portable toilets?

Our quotes are all-inclusive, meaning the price you are quoted will include the delivery, placement, scheduled servicing, final pickup, and all waste disposal fees. There are no hidden charges.

What is your policy if a unit is damaged or graffitied at our site?

We understand that things can happen. Please notify us immediately if a unit is damaged. Our rental agreement details the client's responsibility for damages, but our priority is to promptly service or swap out the unit to ensure you continue to have a functional, clean facility.
